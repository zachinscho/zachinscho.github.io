Home Genius

A custom Wordpress portfolio site.

View homegeniusexteriors.com

Home Genius Exteriors needed a modern, high-performance website that could clearly showcase their services, support lead gen, and scale with their rapidly growing number of locations. The new homegeniusexteriors.com was built to highlight services, establish trust through clear messaging and visuals, and streamline customer inquiries through intuitive navigation and conversion-focused design. The result is a fast, responsive, and polished user experience that supports both brand growth and customer engagement.

Custom Wordpress component based theme using ACF Flexible Content and Timber/Twig. Developed while working at Duckpin. The site features many advanced features including a multi-step form, a careers portal and a locations gmap.
